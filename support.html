Frequently asked questions

Do my photos leave my device?

No. Dusty analyzes your photos and videos entirely on your iPhone. Nothing is uploaded, shared, or seen by anyone. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Will Dusty delete anything automatically?

Never. You always review what will be removed and confirm before anything is deleted. The best photo in a group of similar shots is never pre-selected for deletion.

What does Premium include?

Premium unlocks unlimited cleaning, video compression, swipe mode, screenshot categories and smart selection. You can scan and see your results for free.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open Dusty, go to Settings and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to buy.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. On your iPhone, open Settings › [your name] › Subscriptions, select Dusty and choose your option. You can cancel anytime.

How do I change the app language?

Open Dusty, go to Settings › Language and pick any of the supported languages, or choose Automatic to follow your device language.
